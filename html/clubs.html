Clubs

National Service Scheme (NSS):
The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a government-sponsored youth program in India aimed at fostering social consciousness and community service among college and university students. NSS volunteers actively participate in various activities such as tree plantation, blood donation camps, awareness campaigns on health and hygiene, and community development projects. Through NSS, students not only contribute positively to society but also gain valuable life skills and a sense of responsibility towards the welfare of others.

National Cadet Corps (NCC):
The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth organization in India that aims to develop character, discipline, and leadership qualities among the youth. NCC activities include military training, adventure sports, and community service. NCC cadets have the opportunity to participate in activities like camping, trekking, and parades, which instill a sense of patriotism and national pride. This organization plays a vital role in shaping young minds and nurturing future leaders with a strong sense of duty and service to the nation.
